Security Policy & Vulnerability Disclosure Program

Last updated: March 25, 2026

Reporting a Vulnerability

If you discover a security vulnerability in IRM Sentinel, we encourage responsible disclosure. Please report it to:

Email: security@irmsentinel.com

We aim to acknowledge receipt within 24 hours and provide an initial assessment within 72 hours.

Scope

The following are in scope for our vulnerability disclosure program:

In ScopeOut of Scope
Authentication and authorization bypasses Social engineering or phishing attacks
Cross-site scripting (XSS), CSRF Denial of service (DoS/DDoS)
SQL injection, data exposure Physical security
Tenant isolation failures Vulnerabilities in third-party services
AI prompt injection or data leakage Issues requiring physical access to devices

Safe Harbor

We will not pursue legal action against researchers who:

  • Report vulnerabilities in good faith
  • Do not access or modify data belonging to other users
  • Do not disrupt the availability of our services
  • Allow reasonable time for remediation before public disclosure
